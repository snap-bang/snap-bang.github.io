Welcome to SnapBang: Share Moments, Stay Connected

Welcome to the world of SnapBang, where every moment is a chance to share, connect, and express yourself. Snap empowers you to capture life’s fleeting moments and share them with friends in a way that’s spontaneous, authentic, and fun.

Snap and Share Instantly

Create and share Snaps – photos and videos that disappear after being viewed. Whether it’s a funny face, a breathtaking sunset, or a behind-the-scenes look at your day, Snaps capture the essence of the moment like never before.

Stories: Your Personal Canvas

Build your own Stories by stringing together Snaps into a narrative that lasts for 24 hours. Share your day, your way, and let your personality shine through with a dynamic and evolving Story.

Connect in Real Time

Stay connected with friends through private one-on-one chats or gather your crew in a Group Chat. Express yourself with Bitmojis, stickers, and more, making every conversation uniquely yours.

Video and Voice Calls: Share the Moment

Turn a chat into a live moment with video and voice calls. Share your reactions, celebrations, and everyday experiences as if you were in the same room, no matter where you are in the world.

Lenses and Filters: Express Yourself

Have fun with Lenses and Filters that transform your face, add special effects, and let you express yourself in creative and entertaining ways. From silly to stunning, there’s a Lens for every mood.

Discover: Find What Inspires You

Explore a curated feed of content from friends, creators, and publishers on Discover. Stay up-to-date with the latest trends, news, and entertainment, all in one place.

Control Your Story

SnapBang is built on the idea that what happens on the app stays on the app. With disappearing content and privacy features, you have control over who sees your Snaps and who you connect with.

Safety First: Our Commitment to You

We’re committed to creating a safe and positive space for everyone. Our privacy and safety features are designed to ensure that your experience on SnapBang is enjoyable and secure.
